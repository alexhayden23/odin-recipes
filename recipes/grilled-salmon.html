Grilled Salmon

A rich and delightful marinade really makes this salmon come together

  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 lemon, juiced
  • 1/2 tsp grated ginger
  • 2 tbsp honey
  • 1/3 cup chopped fresh basil
  • 1 tbsp finely chopped shallots
  • 1 3lbs salmon filet with skin
  1. Create a pan with layers of foil large enough to hold the filet. Place the foil on a baking sheet. Lay the filet on the foil, skin side down.
  2. In a small bowl, stir together the oil, soy sauce Worcestershire sauce, lemon juice, ginger, honey, basil and shallots. Pour over salmon and let marinate while preheating the grill, about twenty minutes.
  3. Preheat the grill for medium-low heat. Slide the foil with the salmon off the baking sheet and on to the grill. Cover the grill with the lid and grill the filet for ten minutes per side, roughly twenty minutes. The salmon should be flakable with a fork.
